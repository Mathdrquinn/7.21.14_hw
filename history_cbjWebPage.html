About the Columbus Blue Jackets

The National Hockey League (NHL) is broken into two conferences:
  • Eastern Conference
  • Western Conference
Columbus historically resided in the Western Conference, but with the addition of the Winnepeg Jets in the 2013-2014 season, the Jackets were moved into the Eastern Conference. Within the Eastern Conference are two divisions:
  • Metropolitan Division
  • Atlantic Division
